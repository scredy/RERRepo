Who is Arthur Morgan?

Arthur Morgan is the playable protagonist of Red Dead Redemption 2 — a veteran outlaw of the Van der Linde gang known for his dry wit, unwavering loyalty, and evolving moral compass. Over the course of the story, Arthur confronts loyalty, loss, and redemption while the age of outlaws fades around him.

Role

Senior member & enforcer for the Van der Linde gang; mentor to younger outlaws, scout, and survivalist.

Skills

Exceptional marksmanship, tracking, hunting, horse riding, and a surprising knack for sketching in his journal.

“We can’t fight nature, we can’t fight change, we can’t fight gravity. We can only fight ourselves.” — Arthur Morgan
